Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your tast buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberies, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the faccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oliv eoil, plus more the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medim yellow onions, thintly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a painting knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
